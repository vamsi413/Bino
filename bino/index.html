WHAT IS SWARM?

SWARM is a comprehensive HR and Workforce Management and Automation Software suite combining a multitude of employee management functionalities like internal communication, bug and issue tracking, timesheet management, invoicing, finance, and asset management.

SWARM can be used to send alerts and reminders to the employees and the administrative staff of the organization regarding expiring documents, overdue timesheets and invoices, incomplete employee data and bug tracking. It can also track employee onboarding and immigration status.
